What is COVID-19?

COVID-19 is a virus, which originated in Wuhan, China in 2019. It has since become a global pandemic and there are many concerns regarding what is contributing to the spread of COVID-19. Of those concerns, temperature and particulate matter levels were the ones chosen for this analysis.
